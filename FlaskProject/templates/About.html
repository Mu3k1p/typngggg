About TypeNot

TypeNot is an interactive lyric-typing platform designed to make improving your typing skills more fun, engaging, and meaningful. Inspired by classic typing games but powered by music, TypeNot allows users to type along to real song lyrics while tracking their speed, accuracy, and rhythm. Whether you're a beginner learning to touch type or a music lover looking for a new challenge, TypeNot offers multiple modes—from guided practice with visible lyrics to immersive sessions with just the audio. Developed by students for students and teachers, our goal is to turn boring typing drills into a high-energy, educational experience. Join us in reimagining typing—one beat, one lyric, one keystroke at a time..
